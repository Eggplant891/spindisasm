--- Sequencer Command Notes ---

$11YY 00XX - Play GEMS audio -> Sequencer_CallGEMSFunction in jump table located at $000F459A
YY -> Command
    -> 00 -> Play Once
    -> 01 -> Stop
XX -> Audio bank target

$0009B12C - 2 Longs for YPos and then XPos of sparkle stars that follow plane. Seems to affect spawning of all of them
